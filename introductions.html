I AM A STORY TELLER.

Its great to meet you here. Out of all possibilties, we are here, together. As you have seen (or are about to see) my work thrives across packaging, brand identity, and visual communication. All projects should begin with the need to find the narrative. An entity, an object, a system is only as strong as the story it tells and even more so, the manner in which that story creates emotion within us all.

My methods and processes are due to a big factor in my life. I am co-owner of BLLCRW. I lead our visual identity, garment design, manufacturer relations, and brand storytelling. Bringing to life a creative label has given me the possibility to turn sketches and ideas into shared moments for my team and I, as well as, mold an entity that will exist far after I am no longer here. I create because there will always be a story worth telling, emotions worth discovering, and ideas worth sharing.

I believe it is my purpose is to create with my existence work that resonates, inspires, and communicates with intent.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, After Effects, Lightroom, Procreate, SolidWorks, Keyshot, and Cinema 4D.
